# Privacy Policy

**Glowera**
Last updated: April 15, 2026

---

We built Glowera to be a calm, private space for your wellness journey. This policy explains exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control of it. We'll always keep this plain and honest — no legalese.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Glowera is a wellness app created to help you build gentle habits, track your self-care rituals, and reflect on your growth. For questions about this policy, reach us at **hello@gloweraapp.co**.

---

## 2. What We Collect

### Information You Give Us

**Account information**
When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name (your garden name — which doesn't need to be your real name).

**Wellness content you create**
Everything you add to Glowera stays yours. This includes:
- Habits and completion logs
- Daily and weekly reflections (text)
- Gratitude entries
- Journal entries and voice reflections (audio stays on your device only — it is never uploaded)
- Beauty rituals and completions
- Supplement and GlowStack entries
- Phone-free session logs
- Mood check-ins

**Preferences and settings**
Your notification times, chosen reminder preferences, wellness focus areas, selected rituals, and app theme.

**Community content** *(if you join a pod)*
Display names within pods and messages you post. Other pod members can see this content.

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage and progress data**
Points earned, plant growth stage, streak counts, and milestone achievements — used to power the garden and gamification features.

**Purchase and subscription status**
When you subscribe, RevenueCat (our payment processor) records your subscription status and purchase history. We receive only whether you have active access — not your full payment details, which are handled by Apple.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Your camera or photos
- Health app data (Apple Health, Google Fit)
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Any data for advertising purposes

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| What we use | Why |
|---|---|
| Email address | To create and secure your account, and to contact you if needed |
| Wellness content (habits, reflections, rituals, etc.) | To power your personal tracking, history, and insights inside the app |
| Voice reflections | Processed and stored locally on your device only — never uploaded |
| Points, streaks, plant data | To run the garden growth and gamification system |
| Notification preferences | To send your chosen morning and evening reminders |
| Subscription status | To unlock premium features |
| Pod content | To enable community features within your pod |

We do not use your personal data to train AI models, sell to advertisers, or build behavioral profiles for third parties.

---

## 4. Who We Share Your Information With

We share data only with the services that make Glowera work:

**Supabase** — our database and authentication provider. Your account, wellness content, and habits are stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase enforces row-level security, meaning your data is technically isolated from all other users. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

**RevenueCat** — our subscription management provider. When you subscribe, RevenueCat processes your transaction through Apple and stores your subscription status and purchase history. RevenueCat does not receive your wellness content or journal entries. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

**Apple** — processes all in-app purchases and subscription billing directly. Apple's privacy practices apply to payment transactions. [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Full stop.

---

## 5. Voice Reflections

If you use the voice recording feature, your audio is saved directly to your device's local storage. It is not transmitted to our servers, not processed by any speech recognition service, and not shared with anyone. If you delete the app, the files are removed with it.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
- Row-level security policies that prevent any user from accessing another user's data
- Secure, hashed authentication sessions

No system is perfectly secure. We'll notify you promptly if a breach affects your data.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Settings → Profile → Delete Account), all your data is permanently and immediately deleted from our servers — habits, reflections, journal entries, points, everything. This cannot be undone.

Voice memo files stored locally on your device are removed when you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You are always in control:

- **Access your data** — everything you've entered is visible to you inside the app
- **Edit or delete individual entries** — directly within each feature
- **Delete your account** — go to Profile → Delete Account. This erases all your data permanently
- **Opt out of notifications** — toggle off in Profile → Notifications or in your device Settings
- **Revoke microphone access** — go to your device Settings → Glowera → Microphone

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you may also have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA — including the right to request a copy of your data or object to processing. Contact us at **hello@gloweraapp.co** to exercise these rights.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Glowera is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
